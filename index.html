Currently I am pursuing a master's degree in computer science at Brigham Young University (BYU) under the advisement of Dr. Kevin D. Seppi. My research centers on empowering designers of physicomps—everyday devices enhanced with interactive computing power. By building a piece of software which learns how to perform the signal processing necessary for event recognition, I hope to let the physicomp designer better focus on designing.
