About Articles Elsewhere

I am a proud husband and father; I love my family. My best and dearest friend is my wife. Feeling my little boy wrap his tiny fingers around my pinky as we stroll through a park and hearing my daughter exuberantly sing bring me the greatest joy. Most of my time and energy I devote to providing material necessities for them.

To provide for them, I pursue my twin passions: optimization and automation. When I make a decision, I like to thoroughly study the options and select the right one the first time. If an automaton can perform a tedious task, I would much rather write the algorithm for the automaton to execute than repeatedly perform the task myself. Hence painstakingly engineering data-management and -driven systems is my trade.

I was born and raised in Utah, shared the gospel of Jesus Christ full time in Jamaica, and then completed a bachelor's degree in mathematics at Brigham Young University. While I was completing my undergraduate studies, I met and married my gorgeous wife.
